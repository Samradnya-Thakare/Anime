Naruto: Shippuden

let's have a look on world's one of the most watched japanese webseries Naruto: Shippuden

Naruto:shippuden is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.The story is told in two parts: the first is set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens. The series is based on two one-shot manga by Kishimoto: Karakuri (1995), which earned Kishimoto an honorable mention in Shueisha's monthly Hop Step Award the following year, and Naruto (1997).

Meet the characters of this webseries

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the title character and main protagonist of the franchise. Naruto is a member of a Team 7.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is Naruto's rival and best friend.Sasuke's older brother, Itachi Uchiha, killed the rest of their family. he is a member of Team 7.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is the easygoing, smart leader of team 7, consisting of Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno is a member of Team 7. As a child, Sakura was teased by other children because of her large forehead.

Hinata Hyuga

Hinata Hyuga is a member of Team 8 who suffers from a lack of self-confidence.
